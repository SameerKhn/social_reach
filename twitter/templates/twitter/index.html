What Is Social Reach?

The purpose of SocialReach is to submit the project as a Final year project. The main purpose of this project is that it gives us the power to get complete insights of a user's activity on his twitter's timeline within seconds which anyone is not able to examine just by watching their profiles.

How To Use It?

You just have to enter a valid username of a valid twitter user in the above search bar and within seconds you will pe provided with the latest realtime insights of that user.

Objectives/Deliverables Of SocialReach

Freinds & Followers: Its very interesting that SocialReach shows you the complete insights of followers and freinds that how many your followers are your mutual freinds and whom are not following you back and whom you are not following.

Influence & Engagement: SocialReach provides you the hidden insights of User's engagement with other users, it shows you the maximum followers you could reach by One-Degree connection as well as the average of how many times you tweet has been retweeted or favourited.

Top 10: How much interesting it is that with the help of SocialReach you can easily get the top 10 hashtags used by user,top 10 words used by user as well as top 10 users mentioned by the user.
